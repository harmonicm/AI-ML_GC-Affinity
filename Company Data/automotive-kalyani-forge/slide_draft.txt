PROJECT CODE NAME: Project Orion

SLIDE 1 – Business Overview & Infrastructure

Business Description:
- A leading India-based engineering manufacturer specializing in forged and machined components for automotive and industrial applications.


Product & Service Segments:
- Engine and driveline components for automotive applications
- Industrial forgings serving infrastructure and heavy engineering sectors
- End-to-end manufacturing services including forging, machining, and heat treatment

End-user Industries:
- Automotive OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers
- Industrial, infrastructure, and construction equipment manufacturers
- Power, railways, and agricultural equipment sectors

Manufacturing Footprint:
- Five manufacturing plants located in Western India
- Facilities strategically positioned near key automotive and industrial hubs

Certifications & Quality:
- IATF 16949 certified manufacturing systems
- ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certified operations
- Strong quality, safety, and environmental compliance standards


SLIDE 2 – Financial & Operational Scale

Financial Scale & Growth:
- Established revenue base with operations scaling to ~₹2,300–2,600 Cr in recent years
- Recovery-led growth post-COVID with improving operating performance

Profitability & Returns:
- Improving profitability with EBITDA and PAT rebounding to multi-year highs
- Healthy return ratios supported by operating leverage and cost controls

Operational Metrics:
- Manufacturing operations supported by a workforce of ~500 employees across 5 plants
- Long-standing relationships with global and domestic OEM customers
- Stable credit profile supported by established banking relationships

Growth & Capex Initiatives:
- Ongoing capex program to expand capacity and enhance manufacturing capabilities
- Strong order inflows providing medium-term revenue visibility
- Strategic focus on increasing export contribution and higher-value programs


SLIDE 3 – Investment Highlights

Key Investment Highlights:
- Established market position backed by over four decades of experience in forged and machined components, serving leading global and domestic OEMs
- Scalable manufacturing platform with multi-plant operations and ongoing capacity expansion initiatives
- Improving financial performance with profitability recovering to multi-year highs, supported by operating leverage and disciplined cost management
- High entry barriers driven by stringent quality certifications, long-standing customer relationships, and strong order inflows providing medium-term growth visibility
